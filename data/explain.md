> **About {config.name}**
{config.name} is a bot that, functionally, detects messages matching a certain trigger, then replaces the message under a "pseudo-account" (proxy) using Fluxer webhooks.
Some usage examples of {config.name} includes anything from plural systems, roleplaying, or a host of other activities that might require a proxy.
This bot works through the magic that is Fluxer webhooks. As a result, the proxies will have a `[BOT]` tag next to the name. However, they are not bots, but rather Fluxer users using {config.name}.

For further information, please use `{bot.command_prefix}help`.
