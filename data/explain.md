> **About {config.name}**
{config.name} is a {context.platform} bot that, functionally, detects messages matching a certain trigger, then replaces the message under a "pseudo-account" (proxy) using {context.platform} webhooks.
Some usage examples of {config.name} includes anything from plural systems, roleplaying, or a host of other activities that might require a proxy.
This bot works through the magic that is {context.platform} webhooks. As a result, the proxies will have a `[BOT]` tag next to the name. However, they are not bots, but rather {context.platform} users using {config.name}.

For further information, please use `{config.prefixes[0]}help`.
